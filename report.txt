 Methodology
Data Cleaning and Preparation

Removed duplicates and null entries.

Formatted date and currency fields.

Merged datasets using keys (Product ID, Store ID, etc.).

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Univariate and bivariate analysis.

Visualization of trends using bar charts, time series plots, and heatmaps.

Statistical Analysis and Forecasting

Seasonal decomposition of time series.

Applied ARIMA and Exponential Smoothing models for forecasting.

Dashboards and Reporting

Created interactive dashboards using Power BI/Tableau for visualization.
